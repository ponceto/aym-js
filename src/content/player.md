## AYM路PLAYER

AYM路Player is a [AY-3-8910 / YM2149](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/General_Instrument_AY-3-8910) music player.

The player is made up of one emulated chip and is capable of playing AY/YM musics.

It is based on the AYM路JS emulator and the [Web Audio API](https://developer.mozilla.org/fr/docs/Web/API/Web_Audio_API).

### The player

Just put on your headphones, click `Power` and enjoy listening to old chiptune music from the 80s!
